Jim Godfrey for Mayor of Cambridge

Commitment to our community

In order to provide a proud and thriving community, we need reliable, innovative ideas. I feel compelled to run for mayor to make those ideas a reality. I will bring fresh leadership that will boost our local businesses, strengthen our community, support our working families, and responsibly build and maintain our infrastructure. We must work with all levels of government to lower property taxes, to create good jobs, and to solve our most pressing problems.
