Commitment to Cambridge

I'm Jim Godfrey. As Mayor, I focus on our citizens and our future while safeguarding those qualities which make Cambridge a great place to raise a family. Fortunately, I work with great people. Our track record proves that a forward-looking vision delivers real results for Cambridge.

Our Progress So Far and Our Goals for the Future

  • Tax Rate Reduction: Worked collaboratively to slash the city tax rate by 33% and will continue lowering our tax rate.
  • Public Safety: Championing professional police, public works, and fire departments. Expanded our dedicated teams to 17 police officers and 32 firefighters.
  • Jobs, Housing and Investment Value: Tackling the housing and valuation crisis to ensure every family has a decent place to live in our safe community. Secured over $200 million value in private investments for new local housing construction, continuing to attract new businesses to Cambridge.
  • Infrastructure and Economic Growth: Welcoming a new Veterans Administration clinic, Chipotle, and other small businesses under construction. Collaborated with local stakeholders, state legislators, and Congress to faithfully upgrade our trails, parks, library, and water treatment systems. Developing the Highway 95 Corridor of Commerce to anchor regional growth, and improving infrastructure to ensure storm waters stop flooding our downtown.
  • Major Retail and Small Business Support: Attracted new businesses as well as re-investment in current businesses that meet the needs of our community. Maintaining Cambridge as a premier incubator for entrepreneurs to launch and expand their dreams. Working to draw destination retailers like Costco, Sam's Club, Scheels, or Cabela's to join our successful environment.
  • Youth & Community: Maintained and improved our City Parks while creating more activities for our families and children. Working to build a new regional public park including a lakeside beach attraction.

Every citizen in Cambridge deserves a city defined by livability, stability, fairness, integrity, and thoughtful optimism. By nurturing an environment rich with economic development, decent jobs, and safe streets, we will firmly establish Cambridge as Minnesota's Opportunity Community. I would be honored to continue the great work we have started and to earn your vote for Jim Godfrey as Mayor of Cambridge.
